Fun Facts About Boba

  • Boba, also known as bubble tea originated in Taiwan in the 1980s.
  • The "bubbles" in bubble tea are chewy tapioca pearls made from cassava root.
  • Boba tea comes in many flavors, including milk tea, fruit tea, and smoothies.
  • The large straw used for boba drinks is called a "boba straw"
  • Boba has become popular worldwide and can be found in many countries.

Enjoy your boba responsibly!
